I am a crazy dog lady.

Almost everyone I meet or talk to for any length of time hears something about my dogs, so it would be unthinkable to not mention them here.

EMBER

is now seven years old and raced in Iowa under the name "Astarroyalheaven" and was retired at age 2. She was adopted through National Greyhound Adoption Program NGAP in Philadelphia. Ember is a registered Therapy dog who enjoys visiting with residents of retirement and reahabilitaion facilities. Ember loves digging holes in the backyard, trips in the car and her favorite treats are carrots and string cheese.

BILLIE

is now five years old and raced in Florida under the name "Hallo Mrs. Leen". She was forced into retirement at age 3 due to a broken leg. Fortunately for us, she was patched up and placed for adoption through the Charleston chapter of Greyhound Pets of America. Billie loves races around the backyard and her favorite treat is cheese or peanut butter.
